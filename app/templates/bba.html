Bachelor of Business Administration(BBA) :-

BBA at PIET is a unique and exquisite program devised to equip the students with abundant opportunities for sharpening their creative instincts and latent talents through curricular and extra curricular activities. It is designed to develop intellectual and technical skills that lead to the development of managerial and social sensitivity. The aim of the program is to encourage and boost innovative minds to imbibe newer strategies and achieve the edge in a fiercely competitive world. PIET is one of the Top BBA Colleges in Delhi NCR. The Department of BBA at PIET is committed to the all-round development of the students by inspiring and motivating them to face the challenges of life in a very effortless and sporty manner. The program incorporates creativity, discovery and innovativeness in syllabi to make the students self-reliant in their approach to reach at global height. It quite relates to the well-being of the social environment rather than only conforming to the management ethics. The course strives to impart business education and enhance decision making abilities. Our competent faculty guarantees merit and brilliance by motivating the young and enthusiastic professionals for achieving higher objective in globalization. The technical knowledge and managerial skills are enhanced through learning aids such as assignments, projects, group discussions, seminars, intra & inter departmental competitions and many other creative activities. The students are inspired not only to work hard but also to work smartly and to develop analytical skills through the summer training abroad and corporate practical exposure making us one of the Top BBA College in Haryana. The liaison with the industry is well maintained by undertaking real life projects in organizations like Pantaloon Ltd., Future Group, amongst others; conducting workshops for industry like ‘stress management’ for the employees of National Handloom Development Corporation; motivational lecture by Mr. Navin Gulia and Mr. Motilal Verma; and organizing Guest Lectures from Industry like JM Financial Services, Profit Idea Ltd.. We aim to prepare the students as per the industry requirements by arranging events like a Business Quiz conducted by “Business Standard” and arranging lectures on “Entrepreneurship-Business plan and project report preparation” by Prof. Dr. Sanjiv Mittal, Dean-USMS, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. As an attempt of discharging our social responsibility, workshops are organized on themes of ‘Promotion of Women Consultants in India’ sponsored by North India Technical Consultancy Organization Ltd. Nukkad nataks are also staged on various social issues by the students in various rural belts. All these drives make us one of the Top BBA colleges in Haryana.
Eligibility: 10+2 Pass with 50% marks in any stream with English as a compulsory subject from CBSE or any other State Education Board.

Admissions under Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme/ PIO/ FN
The admission under this category will be made as per instructions of AICTE/Haryana State Technical Education Society, Panchkula/KUK.
