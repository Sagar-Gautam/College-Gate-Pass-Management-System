PIET MAESTROS:

PIET organizes ‘Maestros”an inter-institute technical and cultural fest to provide a unique and conceptual learning opportunity to the students.It aims at showcasing the various scientific research and cultural activities, in other words, the fest is the incorporation of creativities, talents and innovations.

PIET QUEST:

A pride Talent-hunt Project PIET is always known for creativity and vibrancy. There is a plethora of events organized annually. PIET Quest is a unique kind of fest organized to recognize and reward the latent talents of Arts, Science and Commerce stream talent from schools, colleges, and polytechnics since 2008.

About us

Located in the National Capital Region, Panipat Institute of Engineering & Technology (PIET) was established by the Vidyapeeth Education. The founders have a clear vision to provide world-class education and research. In venture of this cherished goal, PIET has achieved many milestones of success. Among some of the best Engineering Colleges in Haryana and National Capital Region, PIET stands out a clear leader. A multidisciplinary institute affiliated to Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
